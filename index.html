History of Assam Tea Plantations

In the nineteenth century, British colonial expansion transformed Assam into a tea frontier. Plantation systems reshaped land ownership, labor migration, and local community life.

Land Transformation

Forest and commons were converted into private plantation estates tied to imperial commerce.

Labor Recruitment

Workers were brought through structured recruitment systems, often under coercive and unequal conditions.

Global Consumption Link

Assam tea entered global markets and became a key component of popular blends such as English Breakfast.
